Robert Norman Ross was an American painter and art instructor who created and hosted "The Joy of Painting", an instructional television program that aired from 1983 to 1994.
His mission was to take the mystery out of art so amateur painters could experience the joy of creating a masterpiece.
Bob Ross is known for his signature landscape paintings filled with "happy little trees" He's most famous paintings include the "Mystic Mountain," where he skillfully captured the ethereal beauty of a distant mountain range surrounded by mist.
Steve Ross is Bob Ross’ son. He is an American painting instructor and TV personality. Following in his father’s footsteps, Steve became a painter and helped keep Bob Ross’ painting traditions alive
